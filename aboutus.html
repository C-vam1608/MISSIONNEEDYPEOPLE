About Us Page

We are the students, and we do help the needy.

If everyone starts thinking about himself, then who will think about those people who are lying destitute on the road? If you think about the families of other needy people except your family, then it is absolutely right.

Welcome!



My name is Shivam Gupta. I was born and raised in Atarra, Uttar Pradesh. I have done my schooling at Tathagat Gyansthali Senior Secondary School in Atarra (Banda), Uttar Pradesh. At present, I am pursuing a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from St. Aloysius' College (autonomous) in Jabalpur, M.P., and with this, I am running a non-profit organisation (Mission Needy People), where we help needy people. We see now old people, children, and women sitting on the roadside and the outskirts, hungry, with the spark of hope in their eyes that someone will come and help us. We know that many of us want to help them but are unable to do so for various reasons; however, by connecting with our mission-needy people (MNP), you can all help these needy people. You can provide them with food, clothes, donations, and education via our MNP. It's my humble request to all those who are reading this message: please help us in this noble cause.
